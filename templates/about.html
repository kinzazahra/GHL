About GHL Infratech

Established in the year 2020, we are engaged in pipeline laying, testing, and commissioning of Oil & Gas pipelines, City Gas Distribution projects, and Operation & Maintenance services.

Our Company is promoted by Mr. Syed Haider Imam, a technocrat with over 20+ years of experience in pipeline infrastructure. His leadership has driven the company towards excellence, allowing us to operate across multiple states including Karnataka, Maharashtra, Odisha, Telangana, Chandigarh, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh.

Our team consists of highly experienced professionals in technical, operational, and business domains, enabling us to achieve sustainable growth and customer satisfaction.
