About GHL Infratech

Established in 2020, we provide comprehensive erection, testing, and commissioning of Oil & Gas Pipelines, City Gas Distribution Projects, and Operation & Maintenance (O&M) Services.

Our operations are driven by three principal business lines: Cross Country Pipelines, City Gas Distribution (including CNG Stations), and O&M services. We are guided by our Promoter, Mr. Syed Haider Imam, a technocrat with over 3 decades of vast technical experience in hydrocarbon pipelines and CGD projects. His vision and leadership continue to take the company to new heights, ensuring timely execution and international quality.

We are proud of our excellent track record of successful project execution from inception. We take pride in relating our success to our employees for their consistent efforts and dedication, ensuring total quality and customer satisfaction across all business areas.

Pan-India Presence 6+ years of experience across Karnataka, Maharashtra, Odisha, Telangana, Chandigarh, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh.
Trusted Partners Presently working with major PSUs and CGD companies across India, maintaining long-term relationships.
Expert Management Driven by highly experienced personnel in technical, operational, and business development domains.
