Pipeline Infrastructure

We undertake projects for laying pipelines in Cross-Country projects and setting up City Gas Distribution networks, including all associated facilities[cite: 51].

1. Cross-Country Pipeline Laying [cite: 52]

Includes fabrication, erection, installation, testing, and commissioning of underground pipelines. We handle mechanical, civil, structural, electrical, and instrumentation works[cite: 54].

2. City Gas Distribution (CGD) [cite: 56]

Our scope includes laying High Pressure Gas Steel and MDPE pipelines for Industrial, Commercial, and Domestic segments (CNG/PNG)[cite: 57, 61]. We cater to households, hotels, hospitals, and power generation industries[cite: 58, 59, 60].

3. Operation & Maintenance (O&M) [cite: 62]

We provide management services for CGD networks, including emergency repairs, modernization, overhauling, and scheduled shutdowns of existing pipelines[cite: 63, 64, 65].
